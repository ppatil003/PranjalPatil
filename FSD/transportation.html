Available Transportation Options in Pune

  • Swargate Bus Stop

    The Swargate Bus Stop is one of Pune's major bus hubs, located in the heart of the city. It serves as a key junction for several PMPML bus routes, connecting various neighborhoods and major city points. The bus station is well-equipped with facilities like ticket counters, waiting areas, and food stalls.

  • Metro Stations

    Pune Metro, operated by the Pune Metro Rail Corporation, features several lines that ease commuting across the city. Major stations include:

    • Pimpri Chinchwad Station: A key terminal in the northern part of Pune, connecting to the central city.
    • Hinjewadi Station: Located near the IT hubs, facilitating access for professionals working in this area.
    • Swargate Station: A central station providing easy connections to various parts of Pune.

  • Uber Facility

    Pune has widespread Uber coverage, making it convenient to get around the city. Uber offers a range of ride options from budget-friendly to premium services. The app allows for easy booking and tracking of rides, making it a popular choice for both locals and visitors.

  • Pune Railway Station

    Pune Railway Station is a major railway hub in Maharashtra, connecting Pune with major cities across India. It offers a range of services including express trains, passenger trains, and facilities such as waiting rooms, refreshment stalls, and ticket counters.
